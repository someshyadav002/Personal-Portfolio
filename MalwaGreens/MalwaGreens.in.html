Orchard With InterCropping

Orchard With InterCropping is planning long
term orchard with short term dissimilar crops simultaneously on the same piece of land to
improve crop productivity and get early returns.

Protected Cultivation

Protected cultivation is a process of growing
crops in a controlled environment to regularatebr temperature, humidity, light and such other
factors as per requirement of the crop.

Integrated Farming

Integrated Farming System is aggragation of
agriculture with Aquaculture, Livestock [Dairy
Goatry Poultry], Bee Keeping and many more to
be incorporated to achieve best from waste.
